title: "Role-Based Access Control"
slug: role-based-access-control
---

Each Circle Admin has an RBAC dashboard enabling them to assign permissions to other addresses for specific operations (e.g., blacklist merchant, settle dispute). Permissions can be revoked at any time. All operations are fully on-chain with no off-chain validation or manual intervention.
